Our workshop started with an introduction to color plants, then introducing how to harvest, the fermentation process and pasta making, continued on the making of dye vat and learning some tie dye techniques and lastly the dye process. The workshop is about 6 hours per day.

Our workshop is open based on request, but somehow we often do the workshop once a month. We are also open for private workshop for those who wants to know the whole detail process about natural dye, usually private workshop do more than one day.

Not only dyeing, we are also open for painting. All the ink we made naturally by extracting the liquid. Using painting, you can motif your fabric directly and you can see the result at the same time, but not for dyeing technique.

We are also providing the participants with wax to create their own motifs. The wax functions to cover the fabric so the color doesn't get inside.
