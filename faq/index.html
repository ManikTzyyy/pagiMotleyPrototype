Frequently Asked Question

About Pagi Motley & Wastra Pagi

What is Pagi Motley?
Pagi Motley is a company dedicated to promoting art and creativity through natural dyes made from organic materials.
Who is your target audience?
Our primary audience is travelers and tourists who appreciate sustainable, nature-inspired products and art.
What is Wastra Pagi's mission?
Wastra Pagi aims to showcase the beauty of natural dye craftsmanship and support cultural heritage through eco-friendly products.
What products do you offer?
We offer a variety of items, including naturally dyed clothing, accessories, and other artisan crafts made with sustainable methods.

About Product

What are your products made of?
Our products are made using natural dyes sourced from plants, fruits, and other organic materials, ensuring eco-friendliness and sustainability.
What makes your products unique?
Our products combine traditional craftsmanship with modern designs, creating items that are both timeless and environmentally conscious.
Are your products handmade?
Yes, all our products are handcrafted by skilled artisans, ensuring each piece is unique and made with care.
What types of products do you offer?
We offer naturally dyed clothing, scarves, bags, home decor, and other artisan crafts, each reflecting our commitment to quality and sustainability.
Are your products suitable for gifting?
Absolutely! Our products make perfect gifts for those who value sustainability, artistry, and unique designs.

About Payment

What payment methods do you accept?
We currently accept payments through bank transfers. Detailed instructions will be provided during the checkout process.
How do I complete a bank transfer payment?
After placing your order, you'll receive our bank account details. Simply transfer the payment amount and send us the proof of payment via email or WhatsApp.
Are there any additional fees for bank transfers?
Any bank transfer fees depend on your bank's policy. Please confirm with your bank to avoid unexpected charges.
How long does it take to process my payment?
Payments are typically processed within 1-2 business days after we receive the proof of payment. You'll receive a confirmation once the payment is verified.
What should I do if I encounter an issue with the payment?
If you face any issues during payment, please contact our customer service team with the details. We're here to assist you.
Do you provide refunds for canceled orders?
Yes, refunds are available for eligible cancellations. Please refer to our refund policy for more details.
Can I pay using a credit card or other methods?
Currently, we only accept bank transfers. We are working to add more payment options in the future.
What currency do you accept for payments?
All payments must be made in Rupiah, unless stated otherwise during checkout.

About Terms and Conditions

What are your general terms and conditions?
By using our services, you agree to our terms, which include adhering to payment deadlines, proper use of our products, and compliance with local laws.
Can I cancel my order after payment?
Orders can only be canceled before shipment. Cancellation policies and potential fees are detailed in our refund and cancellation policy.
What happens if my order is damaged upon delivery?
If your order is damaged during shipping, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damaged item for resolution.
Do you have a privacy policy?
Yes, we are committed to protecting your personal information. Our privacy policy details how your data is collected, used, and safeguarded.
What are your shipping and delivery terms?
Shipping times and delivery terms vary based on location. Estimated delivery times are provided during checkout. Any delays will be communicated promptly.
Are there any restrictions on product use?
Our products are intended for personal use only and not for resale. Any misuse of the products may void warranties or result in legal action.
How do I resolve a dispute or issue?
For any disputes or issues, please contact our customer service. If unresolved, arbitration or legal actions may be pursued as per applicable laws.
