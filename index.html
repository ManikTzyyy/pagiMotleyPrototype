Our Journey

Founded in 2019 by I Made Andika Putra, a textile expert with over 20 years of experience, Pagi Motley was born in the heart of Bali with a bold mission: to empower local communities and protect our environment. Leaving behind a stable career, Andika set up a small studio to inspire change, encouraging youth to stay in their villages and harness local potential.


Our Purpose

Driven by the desire to reduce harmful chemical use in textiles, Andika witnessed firsthand the dangers these chemicals pose—polluting soil, damaging ecosystems, and impacting human health. Pagi Motley takes a small but impactful step toward a sustainable future with eco-friendly practices and natural dye solutions.


The Name

"Pagi" means morning—a fresh start filled with energy. "Motley" represents diversity and vibrancy. Together,Pagi Motley symbolizes the beginning of a colorful, sustainable journey.


Vision & Mision

Our vision is to become a leading green, sustainable textile company by empowering local communities, ensuring environmental sustainability through mindful harvesting, and sharing the art of plant-based dyes through engaging workshops.
